About Me roadmap

  1. I'm Amin, 24 years old. I was born in Torbat-e Heydariyeh and raised in Birjand, Iran. Started my Computer journey using my brother's PC to learn the basics in Windows 98. I always try to be independent and that's probably my boldest feature.

    Back in high school, I found my passion and started working in that field. After graduating, I decided to dive straight into my career instead of going to university or college.
  2. Back in 2016, I began working as a content creator at a download website, which marked my first encounter with WordPress. During that time, I was also involved in creating an e-commerce for a friend and learning about PrestaShop.

    In July 2017, I ventured into learning web development and secured an internship at Fasleaval. I eventually landed a permanent position there, and three years later, I was promoted to the role of Chief Operating Officer. Simultaneously, I also took on a role as a front-end developer at Gruccia.
  3. I love exploring the world through traveling, backpacking, and hiking. Capturing these adventures with photography is a passion of mine. Most of my photos are taken with my phone and Samsung WB350F.

    When I’m not outdoors, I enjoy playing video games (that's where I got "d0Run" from) and listening to different genres of music. I'm also trying to become a gym rat.
  4. In the near future, I can’t wait to embark on adventures across Iran, exploring its beautiful cities. I’m also excited about visiting a few other countries to meet new people.

    On the work front, I’m eager to dive into the world of programming, learning new languages and mastering back-end development. I’m looking forward to the challenges and opportunities ahead!
